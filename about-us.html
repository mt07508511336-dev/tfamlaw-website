About Thompson Family Law

Our story

A family-run practice, built on trust

Since 2011, we're a family-run law firm based in Glasgow, with offices in Coatbridge, Airdrie and Edinburgh — serving the whole of Scotland, from the Highlands and Islands to the Scottish Borders.

Our vision is simple: to offer clients a high-quality, affordable legal service, without the jargon or the wait. We run a fully paperless office, which means your case is never held up looking for a file — everything is at our fingertips, whenever you call.

What we cover

Services built around real life

Our services include the full range of Family Law services: divorce and separation, child contact and custody issues.

We also have deep experience in Interdict cases, as well as complex Professional Negligence and Medical Negligence claims. Whatever the complexity, our job is to identify the real legal issue quickly, so we can move efficiently towards the best outcome for you.

Meet the team behind the advice

Our solicitors bring decades of combined experience across family law, conveyancing, and civil litigation.
